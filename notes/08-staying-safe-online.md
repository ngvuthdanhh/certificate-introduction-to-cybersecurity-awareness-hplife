# 08 – Staying Safe Online

Cybersecurity is not a one-time action but a continuous habit.

### Key Habits
- Stay informed about new online scams.  
- Keep operating systems and apps up to date.  
- Review account privacy settings regularly.  
- Limit the personal data you share on social media.  
- Educate family and colleagues about cyber risks.

### Continuous Learning
Participate in cybersecurity awareness training and certifications such as:  
- **Fortinet NSE 1–3** (Security Awareness)  
- **Google Cybersecurity Fundamentals**  
- **HP LIFE Digital Skills Series**
