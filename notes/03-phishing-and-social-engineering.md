# 03 – Phishing and Social Engineering

Phishing is a **deceptive tactic** used to trick users into revealing personal or financial information.  
Social engineering focuses on **human manipulation**, exploiting curiosity, fear, or trust.

### Common Forms
- **Email phishing:** Fake messages imitating legitimate companies.  
- **Smishing:** SMS-based phishing.  
- **Vishing:** Voice call scams pretending to be authorities.  
- **Clone phishing:** Duplicate of a trusted email with malicious attachments.

### Defensive Practices
- Always verify the sender’s address and domain.  
- Be cautious of urgent or emotional language.  
- Report suspicious messages to IT or cybersecurity teams.
