Employees play a crucial role in protecting organizational assets and information.

### Key Work Practices
- Lock screens when away from devices.  
- Avoid sharing credentials via chat or email.  
- Follow company IT security policies strictly.  
- Report incidents or phishing attempts immediately.  
- Use only authorized software and cloud platforms.

### Work-from-Home Security
- Use a secure Wi-Fi connection with a strong password.  
- Connect through VPN when accessing company resources.  
- Keep work and personal devices separate.
