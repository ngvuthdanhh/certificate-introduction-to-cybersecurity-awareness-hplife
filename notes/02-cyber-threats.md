Cyber threats exploit weaknesses in systems or user behavior. Understanding them is the first step toward prevention.

### Types of Threats
- **Malware:** Harmful software such as viruses, worms, and Trojans.  
- **Ransomware:** Locks data until a ransom is paid.  
- **Phishing:** Deceptive messages tricking users into sharing sensitive data.  
- **Social Engineering:** Manipulating human trust rather than exploiting technology.  
- **Data Breach:** Unauthorized access or leakage of confidential information.

### Prevention Tips
- Avoid clicking unknown links or downloading suspicious attachments.  
- Use updated antivirus software.  
- Regularly back up important data.
