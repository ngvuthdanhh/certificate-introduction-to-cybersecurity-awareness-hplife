Data protection ensures that personal and organizational information remains secure from loss, corruption, or misuse.

### Core Principles
- **Confidentiality:** Data accessible only to authorized users.  
- **Integrity:** Information remains accurate and unchanged.  
- **Availability:** Data is accessible when needed.

### Good Practices
- Encrypt sensitive files and communications.  
- Back up data to offline or cloud storage regularly.  
- Restrict access to files based on roles and necessity.  
- Shred or permanently delete unneeded data.
