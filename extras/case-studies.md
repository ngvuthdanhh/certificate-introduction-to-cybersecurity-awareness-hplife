# Case Studies: Real-World Cybersecurity Awareness

## Case Study 1: Small Business Data Breach
**Scenario:**  
A local bakery stored customer emails and payment details in plain text on a shared office computer.  
An employee clicked a malicious link in an invoice email, leading to a ransomware infection.

**Lesson:**  
- Always encrypt sensitive files.  
- Educate staff on phishing and suspicious attachments.  
- Keep regular offline backups.

---

## Case Study 2: Social Media Oversharing
**Scenario:**  
A freelancer posted photos of their home office, accidentally revealing passwords on sticky notes in the background.

**Lesson:**  
- Be aware of what appears in public photos.  
- Avoid sharing information that could help attackers guess your credentials.  

---

## Case Study 3: Fake IT Support Scam
**Scenario:**  
An employee received a phone call claiming to be from “company tech support” asking for remote access. They allowed access — resulting in credential theft.

**Lesson:**  
- Always verify who’s contacting you.  
- Never share access credentials without confirmation through official channels.
