# Lab 02: Phishing Awareness Simulation

## Objective
Learn how to identify phishing emails and websites.

## Steps
1. Examine sample phishing emails provided by HP Life (or create mock examples).
2. Identify:
   - Suspicious sender address
   - Urgent tone or threats
   - Inconsistent URLs
3. Hover over links to inspect their destination before clicking.
4. Discuss in a team (or journal entry) how to report phishing attempts.
