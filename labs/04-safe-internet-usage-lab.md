# Lab 04: Safe Internet Usage and Social Media

## Objective
Understand safe browsing practices and responsible online behavior.

## Steps
1. Enable privacy settings in your browser.
2. Clear cookies and history to reduce tracking.
3. Review privacy settings on one social media platform you use.
4. Discuss:
   - What kind of data do you share online?
   - How could that data be misused?
